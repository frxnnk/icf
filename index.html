Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Inverse Cramer Fund?

+

The Inverse Cramer Fund is a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) that combines the fun of memecoins with the promise of DeSci (decentralized science) projects. Inspired by a contrarian approach, we invest in high-risk, high-reward crypto assets while involving the community in key decisions.

How does the fundraising process work?

+

We open a one-week fundraising period where contributors can buy DAO tokens at a fair launch price. The total funds raised will determine the starting capital for our investments. Everyone gets the same token price during this period to ensure fairness.

What can I do with the DAO tokens?

+
  • Voting Power: Influence 20% of the fund's investment decisions.
  • Profit Sharing: At the fund's expiration, redeem profits proportional to your token holdings.
  • Trading Options: You can sell your tokens anytime on the AMM as long as the fund's market cap exceeds the original raise.
